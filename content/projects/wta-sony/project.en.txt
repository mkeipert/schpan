Projectcontent: 

Consulting WTA sponsoring

* Until 2013 Sony was the Lead Global Sponsor of the Women’s Tennis Association. 
* Within the framework of the global sponsorship, Sony Ericsson possessed numerous branding rights, covering nearly all women’s tennis tournaments worldwide. 
*  **Schillinger & Pankratz** advised Sony Ericsson on branding as well as brand positioning that are directly on the courts and ideal for TV coverage. 
* In addition, Schillinger & Pankratz was responsible for the production, coordination and global distribution of all net signage. 
* For the WTA Schillinger & Pankratz is still responsible for the production, coordination and global distribution of all net signage.
