* Global Customer Care Golf tournaments on an annual basis for Mercedes-Benz. 
* 13 highly esteemed, invitation-only tournaments in Germany and a multi-day final form the core of this project.
* **Schillinger & Pankratz** is responsible for the conceptualization and implementation of the events, as well as for providing media coverage.
* Great effort is taken each year via creative processes to ensure that the Mercedes-Benz brand values and products can be rightfully experienced.
